Our Story

Founded in 2025, Bakushin O was born from a passion for sharing authentic Japanese flavors with the world. Our name means "explosive speed" — inspired by the idea of bringing fresh, flavorful dishes quickly to every table without compromising quality.

We began as a small ramen stall in Tokyo, and with dedication, we’ve grown into a full-service restaurant serving sushi, ramen, and tempura — all prepared with care and heart.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Hiroshi Sato leads our kitchen with over 15 years of experience in Japanese fine dining. His philosophy is simple: use the freshest ingredients and respect traditional methods.
